Level 1

AIM SPEED

Provides an increase to the speed with which you aim down sights.

RELOAD SPEED

Provides an increase to the speed of reloading weapons.

HIP SHOT ACCURACY

Lowers the negative spread impact of firing when shooting from the hip.

RUN AND GUN

Lowers the negative spread impact of firing while moving.

WEAPON RECOIL

Provides a decrease to the amount of recoil when firing any weapon.

ARMOR

Provides an increase to your damage resistance.

WEAPON SWAY

Provides a decrease to the amount of weapon sway when aiming.

ARMOR DAMAGE

Increases amount of damage done to enemy armor.

MAKE 'EM COUNT

Single shot fire with automatic weapons does more damage to components.

TRIGGER HAPPY

Increases amount of damage dealt when using automatic fire.

MARKSMAN

While active, the Marksman specialization completely eliminates weapon sway when using any weapon.

Only one specialization may be active at a time.

VANGUARD

While active, the Vanguard specialization increases your damage resistance with 25%.

Only one specialization may be active at a time.

THROW ACCURACY

Provides a percentage decrease to the random offset of your throwing accuracy.

THROW DISTANCE

Provides an increase to the distance you can throw throwable items.

FLANKING

Increased chance of causing stagger when firing on an enemy that is targeting another player.

HEALER

Unlocks the ability to heal other players with a medkit.

ENEMY MARKING

Spotting enemies will highlight them to all players for a short period of time.

HEAL AMOUNT

Increases the healing amount when using medkits.

VETERAN GUERILLA

Marked enemies that are destroyed yield increased amount of base XP.

DESIGNATED TARGET

Marked enemies take additional damage.

LAST MAN STANDING

Provides a boost to your damage resistance for every player (including you) that drops below 10% health. Including downed players.

REVIVE SPEED

Lowers the time it takes to revive a downed teammate.

COMMANDER

While the Commander specialization is active, deployed Field Radios can be used an additional 3 times. (Total of 6 uses).

>Only one specialization may be active at a time.

MEDIC

While the Medic specialization is active, any player you revive, including yourself, return to the game with 100% health.

Only one specialization may be active at a time.
